🃏 The Joker's Successor

The Joker is dead, but his legacy refused to die with him. Shortly after his death, a mysterious phenomenon began spreading throughout Gotham, causing random people—criminals, civilians, even law enforcement—to suffer from uncontrollable laughter, vivid hallucinations of the Joker, and an overwhelming obsession with turning life into a cruel joke. Doctors found no virus, poison, or trace of the Joker's blood, leading to the terrifying realization that what remained was not a disease, but an idea—an infectious madness that could consume anyone vulnerable enough to embrace it. Most victims either recovered or lost their minds completely, but one individual accepted the voice in their head instead of resisting it. Rather than becoming another imitation of the Clown Prince of Crime, they forged their own identity while inheriting his chaotic philosophy, earning the title **"The Joker's Successor."** With a new face, a new mind, and an even more unpredictable vision of chaos, Gotham soon learns that while the original Joker is gone forever, his laughter has found someone worthy of carrying it into a darker age.
